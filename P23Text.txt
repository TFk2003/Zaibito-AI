“12-A.Registration  of  Specialized  Trust.  -  (1)For  the  purpose  of 
registration  of  specialized  trust  under  this  Act,  the  trustee  shall 
provide to the Director a No Objection Certification (NOC) from the 
relevant regulator which shall contain the prescribed information. 

(2)  Where  the  trustee  provides  NOC  under  sub-section  (1),  the 
requirements of sections 13, 14 and 15 shall be deemed to have been 
fulfilled  and  the  Assistant  Director  shall  thereafter  register  the 
Specialized Trust in accordance with section 16. 

(3)All  Specialized  Trusts  created  prior  to  coming  into  force  of  this 
Act shall be deemed to have been registered in accordance with this 
section and shall be issued a Certificate of Registration under section 
16:
